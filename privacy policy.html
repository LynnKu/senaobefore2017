Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to SENAO and always has been. So we’ve developed a Privacy Policy that covers how we collect, use, transfer, and store your information. Please familiarize yourself with our privacy practices before you use the site.

Collection and Use of Personal Information

Personal information is data that can be used to identify or contact a single person.

You may be asked to provide your personal information anytime you are in contact with SENAO. SENAO and its affiliates may share this personal information with each other and use it consistent with this Privacy Policy. They may also combine it with other information to provide and improve our products, services, and content. You are not required to provide the personal information that we have requested, but, if you chose not to do so, in many cases we will not be able to provide you with our products or services or respond to any queries you may have.

What personal information we collect

When you use “Contact us” on this site, SENAO may collect the information you provide such as name, email address, and phone number. SENAO will use such information to fulfill your requests, provide the relevant product or service, or for anti-fraud purposes.

How we use your personal information

We may also use personal information for internal purposes such as auditing, data analysis, and research to improve SENAO’s products, services, and customer communications.

Our Companywide Commitment to Your Privacy

In order to make sure your personal information is secure, we communicate our privacy and security guidelines to SENAO employees and strictly enforce privacy safeguards within the company.

SENAO may update its Privacy Policy from time to time.

When we change the policy in a material way, a notice will be posted on our website along with the updated Privacy Policy.
